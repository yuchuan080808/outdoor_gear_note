Water sports gear has different failure points than general hiking gear: wet storage, drainage, sand cleanup, zipper durability, strap comfort, and space for fins, towels, or masks. This category groups guides for snorkeling, diving, beach carry, and wet-gear organization.

Use these guides when you need outdoor gear that dries quickly, packs awkward equipment cleanly, and survives repeated beach or boat use.
