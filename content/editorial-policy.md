Outdoor Gear Notes publishes marketplace-informed outdoor gear buying guides. Our goal is to help readers understand fit, tradeoffs, setup needs, and buyer cautions before comparing products.

## How We Use Marketplace Data

We review Amazon product-page details, marketplace rating signals, review-count depth, buyer-feedback summaries, and category context. Bestseller status is treated as a popularity signal, not proof of quality.

## Review Boundaries

Unless an article explicitly says otherwise, our guides are not based on hands-on field testing. We do not invent trail tests, durability measurements, waterproof ratings, warmth ratings, lab results, certifications, or warranty terms.

## Buyer Feedback Summaries

User feedback summaries are written to surface patterns: common praise, evidence depth, complaint pressure, and recurring watch-outs. They are not a substitute for reading the latest product page, current reviews, manufacturer instructions, or safety guidance.

## Prices and Availability

Amazon prices, coupons, availability, and product pages can change frequently. We use broad price tiers when useful and avoid live price claims unless exact live data is available.

## Affiliate Disclosure

As an Amazon Associate, Outdoor Gear Notes may earn from qualifying purchases. Amazon links are treated as sponsored links, and our editorial descriptions should still include meaningful cautions, skip-if guidance, and buyer-fit tradeoffs.
